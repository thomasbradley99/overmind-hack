How It Works — Per-Clip Breakdown

OR strategy: if either model says goal, it's a goal. Team from moondream when it detects.
ClipTruthsmolvlm2moondreamEnsemble (OR)TeamCorrect

Why This Works

smolvlm2 never misses a goal (100% recall). It is the "safety net."

moondream is selective but precise. When it detects a goal, the team is always correct.

The OR (Union) strategy means: if either says goal, we trust it. Since smolvlm2 catches everything, recall stays 100%. When moondream also detects, we get its perfect team ID.

Trade-off: We still get 4 false positives (same as smolvlm2 alone), but team accuracy jumps from 60% to 80% because we use moondream's team when both agree.
